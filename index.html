Crop Monitoring System

A Hardware project on IoT Which keeps a track on the crops and steps can be taken to prevent any damage accordingly and maximize the crop yield. Farmers face issues in determining the amount of water required for a crop, tracking crop development on a real-time basis and detecting crop health issues. Following things are in our project to help farmers.
[1] The sensor is attach with the crop which will tell the time when the crop has insufficient water similarly when it needs pesticides, whether the chemical composition is harmful for the crop or not. Which type of pesticides will be the best for that particular crop, etc.
[2] A buzzer or alarm can be attracted to it, which will ring when crop need water
